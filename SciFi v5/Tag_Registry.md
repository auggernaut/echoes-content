## üåå Primary Archetypes

### üåë [LURKER]
*   **Narrative**: Predators that strike from the blind spots of perception. They are the scratch in the vent, the flickering light, and the feeling of being watched.
*   **Themes**: Isolation, Paranoia, Vulnerability.
*   **Suggested Mechanics**: Line-of-sight triggers, ambush boons, environmental hiding.

### üåÄ [SURREAL]
*   **Narrative**: Threats that warp memory, logic, and the laws of physics. They represent the ship itself becoming an unreliable narrator.
*   **Themes**: Identity Loss, Impossible Geometry, Psychological Decay.
*   **Suggested Mechanics**: Echo flips/discards, "Psyche" pools, narrative hallucinations.

### üåø [BIO]
*   **Narrative**: Invasive, wet, and evolutionary horror. Rapid growth, mutation, and the subversion of the biological form.
*   **Themes**: Contagion, Mutation, Visceral Decay.
*   **Suggested Mechanics**: Slow Burn infections, spreading hazards, specialized gear requirements.

### ‚ö° [TECH]
*   **Narrative**: Rogue AIs, automated security, and cold, calculating mechanical logic. The ship's original systems turning against the crew.
*   **Themes**: Clinical Violence, Surveillance, Obsolescence.
*   **Suggested Mechanics**: Rigid Protocols, equipment locks, technical bypasses.
