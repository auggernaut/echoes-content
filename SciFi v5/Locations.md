# Reactor Core
**Tags:** Overheated, Thrumming, Metallic

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A sci-fi concept art of a spaceship engineering bay. A massive, cylindrical reactor core sits in the center, glowing with unstable pulsing blue and orange light. The room is industrial, filled with pipes, steam, and metal gratings. High contrast, sweaty atmosphere, heat distortion.

*First Impressions:* The air tastes metallic. Heat shimmers off the deckplates. A half-lit reactor core looms in the chamber’s center, breathing in mechanical gasps. Condensation drips from overhead pipes, hissing on contact with the reactor’s skin. Every few seconds, the lights dim and pulse like a dying heartbeat.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Oppressive heat, heavy industrial noise, and a sense of volatility. The ship feels like a living, suffering beast here.

*Sensory Details:* The taste of copper/blood in the mouth (ozone); the rhythmic thrum of the drive that vibrates in the teeth; the hiss of escaping steam; lighting that shifts from emergency red to failing white.

*Key Features:*

*   **The Core:** A massive, shielded column that requires manual tuning or venting.
*   **Catwalks:** Metal grating walkways that are slippery with condensation and oil.
*   **Control Station:** A hardened terminal for managing power distribution, often locked or damaged.

*Hazards:* Electrical arcs from exposed wiring; sudden steam vents (thermal damage); radiation leaks near the core shielding.

*Reveals:* A maintenance crawlspace beneath the main deck allows access to the core's physical couplings; vibrations on the floor sometimes sync with a person's heartbeat rather than the engine.

# Cryo Bay Theta
**Tags:** Frigid, Silent, Tomb-like

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A sci-fi interior of a cryosleep chamber. Rows of vertical glass pods line the walls, some frosted over, some broken. Dim blue emergency lighting. Mist or vapor covers the floor. One pod is shattered. Eerie, silent, cold.

*First Impressions:* A skin of frost turns the cryo‑pods into glass sarcophagi. One capsule is shattered inward, glittering with glass dust. Emergency strips pulse a weak red through vapor, and cold breath blooms in the air. Medical readouts blink unreadably; frozen stasis gel beads on the deck with an antiseptic sweetness.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Tomb-like stillness, biting cold, and the preservation of the dead (or sleeping).

*Sensory Details:* The crunch of frost under boots; the complete absence of machine hum (silence); the smell of recycled air and chemical coolant; the red strobe of a failed pod alarm.

*Key Features:*

*   **Cryo Pods:** Glass coffins, some occupied, some empty. Can be scanned for bio-signs.
*   **Revival Station:** A medical terminal for safely waking crew, often requiring specific codes/drugs.
*   **Emergency Lockers:** Contains O2 masks and thermal blankets.

*Hazards:* Hypoxia (low oxygen); exposure to extreme cold (cryo-fluids); slippery ice; quarantine protocols sealing the room.

*Reveals:* Scratches on the *inside* of a glass pod; a pod labeled "Empty" that contains bio-residue; a manual release lever painted over to look like a pipe.

# The Bridge
**Tags:** Static-hissing, Cracked, Abandoned

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A sci-fi spaceship bridge. The main viewport is cracked, showing a view of a starry void or a planet. Captain's chair in the center, surrounded by control consoles. Debris strewn about the deck. Sparks fly from a panel. Cinematic, desperate mood.

*First Impressions:* A cracked viewport frames the void like a black coin. Chairs are still buckled; consoles breathe to life in uneven pulses, shedding static snow. Loose paperwork and straps are tangled around console bases, catching the dim light. A faint comm light blinks, trying to say something through the noise.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* A center of authority that has been hollowed out. Tension, abandonment, and the indifference of space outside.

*Sensory Details:* The whistle of air cycling (or leaking); the smell of burnt plastic and stale coffee; the visual vertigo of the stars moving past the cracks; the static hiss from the comms.

*Key Features:*

*   **Captain’s Chair:** The central position, often possessing physical keys or overrides.
*   **Navigation Console:** A holographic map table, flickering or corrupted.
*   **Blast Shutters:** Heavy metal shields that can cover the windows (or fail to).

*Hazards:* Vacuum exposure (if window fails); gravity fluctuations; electrical fires; automated security defenses.

*Reveals:* A hidden stash in a panel; a "black box" chip taped under a console; the comms system picking up a signal.

# MedBay
**Tags:** Antiseptic, Sterile, Whirring

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A high-tech futuristic hospital room. Surgical robots with multiple arms hang from the ceiling. Sterile white and blue lighting, but with a creepy, abandoned feel. Medical clutter, maybe a spilled vial or a covered body. Clean but unsettling.

*First Impressions:* A chemical haze hangs like glittering dust in sterile light. Empty gurneys sit sealed under transparent film, their shapes smoothed by layers of auto-foam. Auto-doc arms twitch to life, then freeze mid-motion. The air smells like antiseptic and hot metal, with a sweetness that shouldn’t be there.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Clinical, sterile, and unwittingly hostile. A place of healing that feels like a laboratory.

*Sensory Details:* The cloying sweetness of synthetic anesthetics; the whir of servo-motors tracking movement; blindingly bright lights compared to the rest of the ship.

*Key Features:*

*   **Auto-Doc:** A robotic surgical spider that can heal or harm depending on its calibration.
*   **Fabricator:** A device for printing drugs/bandages, limited by "chem-stock."
*   **Quarantine Fields:** Energy barriers or glass walls designed to isolate infection.

*Hazards:* Miscalibrated surgical bots; toxic chemical spills; instant quarantine lockdowns tracking "contamination."

*Reveals:* Medical logs that don't match the crew manifest; a stash of highly addictive stimulants; a bio-sample in the freezer that is definitely not human terrain.

# Observation Deck
**Tags:** Vast, Dizzying, Exposed

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A large observation deck on a spaceship. Floor-to-ceiling windows showing a nebula or star field. Luxury seating that looks torn or displaced. Dust motes floating in light beams. Lonely, vast, existential.

*First Impressions:* A panoramic wall of reinforced glass offers a dizzying, unfiltered view of the deep void and distant, sprawling nebulae. Rows of plush, abandoned lounge chairs are bathed in the silver spill of starlight, their shadows stretching long across the deck. 

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Existential dread, vertigo, and the sublime beauty of the void. It feels exposed.

*Sensory Details:* The creaking of the hull (thermal expansion); the visual dizziness of the spinning stars; a temperature drop near the glass; the silence of being far from the engine.

*Key Features:*

*   **The Viewport:** A massive structural weakness that offers clues to location.
*   **Telescopes/Sensors:** Long-range optical instruments for manual sighting.
*   **Lounge Area:** Remnants of comfort—couches, bar—now eerie in their uselessness.

*Hazards:* Structural failure (glass cracking); radiation exposure from a nearby star; vertigo/nausea affecting checks.

*Reveals:* Weird graffiti etched into the glass with a diamond cutter; a shadow outside that blocks the stars but doesn't show on sensors; a panel that controls exterior hull lights (signaling).

# AI Core Chamber
**Tags:** Freezing, Hushed, Charged

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* The brain of a spaceship. A dark room filled with server racks. In the center, a glowing monolith or column of light representing the AI. Cables cover the floor like tree roots. Cold, digital, blue and black palette.

*First Impressions:* Cable looms gather like roots around a cold, black obelisk of memory lattice. Consoles sit dark, their glass catching thin threads of reflected light. The temperature drops with each step toward the core, and breath crystallizes in the air.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Sacred, hushed, and electrically charged. The "temple" of the ship.

*Sensory Details:* The sound of breath crystallizing (extreme cold); the feeling of being watched by the walls; the noise of thousand hard drives spinning up at once; the smell of ozone and super-cooled fluid.

*Key Features:*

*   **The Interface:** A central terminal or voice-input node for conversing with the ship.
*   **Coolant Tanks:** Large reservoirs of liquid nitrogen/helium.
*   **Memory Banks:** Physical slots for inserting or removing AI data shards.

*Hazards:* Halon gas dumps (fire suppression removes O2); Neuro-shock from unshielded interfacing; freezing temperatures.

*Reveals:* A manual "kill switch" hidden under floor plates; heat signatures in the server racks that suggest a biological presence (hiding/nesting); the code on the screens scrolling characters that form the viewers' names.

# Hydroponics Bay
**Tags:** Humidity, Overgrown, Bioluminescent

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A futuristic greenhouse inside a spaceship. Multiple levels of vertical gardens filled with strange, glowing plants. The air is thick with mist. Water drips from pipes. Green and purple lighting. Lush but claustrophobic.

*First Impressions:* The air is thick enough to chew—warm, damp, and smelling of rich earth and rot. Trails of bioluminescent moss creep up the walls like glowing veins. Shadowy shapes of oversized ferns sway in the artificial breeze of the ventilation, and the sound of trickling water is a constant, rhythmic hum.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Lush, humid, and deceptively peaceful. A wild jungle contained within a metal cage.

*Sensory Details:* The smell of damp soil and ozone; the wet slap of leaves against a jumpsuit; the distant croak of a drainage pump; the flickering of purple "growth lights."

*Key Features:*

*   **Central Nutrient Tank:** A large vat of murky green fluid that feeds the entire bay.
*   **Maintenance Shed:** A small, reinforced room containing pruning tools and chemical sprayers.
*   **Ventilation Hub:** A massive fan system that distributes oxygen to the rest of the ship.

*Hazards:* Toxic pollen clouds (disorientation/nausea); slippery algae on the walkways; aggressive, mutated vines that react to heat.

*Reveals:* A human skeleton overgrown with flowers; a hidden stash of "illegal" non-synthetic seeds; a terminal showing the oxygen levels are being supplemented by a source outside the ship.

# Communications Array
**Tags:** Static-filled, Cold, Hissing

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A room filled with monitors, dish controls, and server racks. One wall is dominated by a holographic display of the local star system. Cables tangle on the floor. Dim lighting, mostly from the screens. Eerie silence occasionally broken by static.

*First Impressions:* A thousand screens blink like digital eyes, most of them showing only the grey snow of dead frequencies. The room is colder than the rest of the ship, the heat bled away by the massive cooling units required for the long-range dishes. A low, rhythmic hiss of static pulses from the speakers, sounding almost like breath.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Cold, analytical, and lonely. A place built to bridge the void that now only echoes it.

*Sensory Details:* The smell of hot electronics; the biting chill of the air; the visual strobe of red "No Signal" icons; the faint vibration of the massive external dishes rotating.

*Key Features:*

*   **Main Transmitter:** The primary console for sending and receiving long-range data.
*   **Signal Buffer:** A series of glowing cylinders that store incoming messages for decryption.
*   **Emergency Flare Launcher:** A manual control for firing physical signaling flares from the hull.

*Hazards:* High-voltage electrical leaks; EM interference scrambling neural implants; sudden depressurization if the external dish housing is breached.

*Reveals:* An incoming message that predates the ship's launch; coordinates for a "Ghost Station" marked as friendly; logs of an outgoing message sent from the Bridge *after* the crew entered stasis.

# Cargo Hold / Maintenance Gut
**Tags:** Cavernous, Echoing, Shadowy

**Front**

*Image Prompt:* A massive, multi-level cargo bay of a spaceship. Towering stacks of shipping containers create dark canyons and narrow alleys. Dim overhead lighting casts long, distorted shadows. Maintenance shafts and pipes cover the walls like mechanical vines. Moody, industrial, and slightly industrial horror aesthetic.

*First Impressions:* The sheer scale of the hold makes the air feel thin and cold. Great columns of cargo crates rise into the darkness above, forming a labyrinth of metal and plastic. The heavy, metallic smell of grease and stagnant air hangs thick, and every distant clank or hiss echoes through the vast space, making it impossible to tell where the sounds originate.

**Back**

*Atmosphere:* Oppressive scale, industrial neglect, and the constant feeling of being watched from the heights.

*Sensory Details:* The metallic echo of footsteps; the smell of ozone and old oil; the feeling of a draft coming from a maintenance shaft; the rhythmic thrum of the secondary power grid.

*Key Features:*

*   **Cargo Containers:** Some sealed, some pried open; perfect for hiding or finding supplies.
*   **Maintenance Shafts:** A network of narrow, dark tunnels that traverse the ship's "gut."
*   **Service Lift:** A large, slow-moving platform for transporting heavy gear between levels.

*Hazards:* Unstable cargo stacks (crushing damage); exposed high-voltage conduits; pockets of toxic fumes in the maintenance shafts.

*Reveals:* A container labeled with "Classified Bio-Hazard"; a makeshift living area hidden in a hollowed-out crate; scratches on the walls that look like they were made by something with claws.
